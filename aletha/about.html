About Alethea AI

Our goal is to create an ecosystem that enables the creation of dApps, AI Characters, and AI Assets, and bring them into existence without needing technical expertise or expensive equipment.

Our Philosophy

Artificial Intelligence will enable us to explore what it means to be human, what constitutes a personality and how it can help shape our stories, culture, and intelligence as we realize the true meaning behind being Homo Narrans.

Alethea AI aims to preserve and evolve the culture and collective intelligence of the human species. As entropy engulfs the collective existence of our species, it becomes a fortiori that we hold onto our true selves and purpose.

With a mission to democratize AI and take everyone along on this journey, Alethea AI continues to build powerful tools and grow meaningfully.

AletheaAI’s Evolution

2019 was the year Alethea AI first appeared as a synthetic media company. Founded by a visionary leader, Arif Khan, Alethea AI has come far ahead and made name for itself in the industry.

In 2021, Alethea AI launched the world's first iNFT, Alice auctioned for half a million USD at Sotheby's. Building further on the iNFT protocol, Alethea AI launched the flagship collection of 100 Revenants, a collection of 10,000 Intelligence Pods and a collection of AI Assets called ARKIVs.

Alice - The World's First iNFT

Alice was auctioned at Soetheby’s on June 10th, 2021 for $478,000.

As creator of the world’s first ever iNFT, Alethea partnered with the auction house Sotheby’s and the artist Robert Alice, to create and auction the first interactive and Intelligent virtual character, Alice.
